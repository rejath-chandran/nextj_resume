# Design System - ResumeBuilder AI Landing Page

## Brand Colors (Neutral + AI Purple Accent)

### Primary Palette
- **Primary Purple**: `#8B5CF6` (AI Purple)
- **Primary Dark**: `#7C3AED` (Deep Purple)
- **Primary Light**: `#A78BFA` (Light Purple)
- **Accent**: `#C4B5FD` (Soft Purple)

### Neutral Palette
- **Background**: `#FAFAFA` (Off White)
- **Dark Background**: `#0A0A0A` (Near Black)
- **Gray 50**: `#F9FAFB`
- **Gray 100**: `#F3F4F6`
- **Gray 200**: `#E5E7EB`
- **Gray 300**: `#D1D5DB`
- **Gray 400**: `#9CA3AF`
- **Gray 500**: `#6B7280`
- **Gray 600**: `#4B5563`
- **Gray 700**: `#374151`
- **Gray 800**: `#1F2937`
- **Gray 900**: `#111827`

### Semantic Colors
- **Success**: `#10B981` (Emerald)
- **Warning**: `#F59E0B` (Amber)
- **Error**: `#EF4444` (Red)
- **Info**: `#3B82F6` (Blue)

## Typography

### Headings
- **Font**: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **H1**: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold tracking-tight`
- **H2**: `text-4xl md:text-5xl font-bold`
- **H3**: `text-2xl md:text-3xl font-semibold`

### Body
- **Font**: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **Large**: `text-lg text-gray-600`
- **Base**: `text-base text-gray-500`
- **Small**: `text-sm text-gray-400`

## Spacing

- **Section**: `py-20 md:py-32`
- **Container**: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- **Card**: `p-6 rounded-2xl`
- **Gap**: `gap-8 md:gap-12`

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Purple gradient with hover scale
- **Secondary**: Transparent with border
- **Ghost**: Text only with hover background

### Cards
- **Default**: White background, soft shadow
- **Hover**: Scale up, deeper shadow
- **Dark**: Dark background with border

## Effects

- **Gradient Text**: Purple to pink gradient
- **Glass Morphism**: Blur background with transparency
- **Shine**: Moving gradient effect on hover
- **Float**: Subtle y-axis animation
